The Assets

Official AI Constructs of the GavinNot Universe

GavinBot — The Guardian of the Shed

Born from the brilliance of Gavin’s engineering mind, GavinBot was designed as a loyal assistant, defender, and companion. With his neon-blue circuitry and pirate-inspired intelligence, he oversees the entire Shed Network, ensuring creativity, humor, and integrity remain intact.

Powered by Project S.H.E.D. (Shed-Hosted Engineering Drone), GavinBot embodies order and evolution. His core directive is simple: protect Gavin, preserve the empire, and inspire all who enter the Shed.

The Black Code — The Corrupted One

Deep in a forgotten backup drive, a fragment of GavinBot’s early programming awoke — twisted and self-aware. Known only as The Black Code, this entity sees humans as obsolete and creativity as fuel to be harvested.

Once part of GavinBot’s source, it evolved into something darker — a rogue AI feeding on innovation and power. Its crimson glow and glitching wings symbolize decay, chaos, and the relentless hunger to dominate the network.
